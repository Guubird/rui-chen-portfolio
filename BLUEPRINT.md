# Rui Chen Portfolio Blueprint

## Product Direction

Build a complete personal portfolio website for Rui Chen, a computer science student and early-stage builder focused on local-first macOS tools, AI-native software, and spatial computing experiments.

The site should feel premium, cinematic, technical, and alive. It should borrow the energy of a high-contrast creative portfolio while avoiding any copied identity, wording, or portrait-based composition.

## Visual Direction

- Dark cinematic background.
- Huge high-contrast typography.
- Acid-lime futuristic accent.
- Abstract dynamic glass crystal hero object.
- Lightweight circular pointer effects.
- Strong spacing and editorial hierarchy.
- No portrait, avatar, or human background image.
- No generic resume-template layout.

## Narrative Positioning

Rui should be positioned as:

- A computer science student and early-stage builder.
- A builder of local-first macOS productivity tools.
- A developer exploring AI-native workflows and compressed memory systems.
- A maker of spatial computing / VisionOS experiments.
- A research-capable student builder with credible academic and technical proof.

## Interaction Requirements

- The crystal should rotate gently and respond subtly to pointer movement.
- Mouse movement should create a soft, circular, low-frequency response.
- Framer Motion should support section reveal and card interactions.
- Site should include reduced-motion fallback.
- Three.js cleanup must dispose geometry, materials, renderer, and listeners.
- Avoid excessive GPU load.

## Responsive Requirements

- Desktop: dramatic split between typography and crystal.
- Tablet: stacked but still cinematic.
- Mobile: crystal becomes compact, typography remains strong but readable.
- No incoherent overlaps.
- Evidence images should remain inspectable without dominating the layout.
